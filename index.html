Rules & Format
To ensure a fair and enjoyable tournament, all participants must adhere to these rules:

- No Emulators Allowed: Play only on official devices.
- No Hacks, Cheats, or Third-Party Tools: Any use will result in immediate disqualification.
- Minimum Player Level Requirement: Must be Level 50 or above in BGMI.
- Room ID & Password: Shared via WhatsApp 10 minutes before each match.
- Disqualification Policy: Violations lead to ban from future events.

Match Format: Squad / TPP (Third-Person Perspective). Points awarded based on survival and eliminations.
